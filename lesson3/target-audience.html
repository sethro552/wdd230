Two Personas

Persona 1

This will be the group of people who live withing the chamber of commerce. They will be able to find information about upcoming events in the community. As per the website all of the important information will be available from it.

Persona 2

This group of people will be visitors from out of town. The could just be visiting someone or passing through on a trip. They could be intrested in any historic things about the area or perhaps just a place to spend the night.

Scenarios

Scenario 1
When is the Flipton cherry pie bakeoff?
This could be found out by checking the community calendar.
Scenario 2
A father driving his family to an amuesment park could be passing through the chamber looking for a motel for the night.
This family could find what lodging is available by simply clicking on the area map located on the homepage of the website.
Scenario 3
Where could someone find information about an energy saving program?
Anyone intrested could check the service department page on the website for that information.
