Site Name

This name of this site will be The Hudson Chamber of Commerce. This is a small town in Ohio which is about 45 minutes away from where I live.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a chamber of commerce for a fictional community. This site will have events and a calendar. There will also be image that will give a visual effect of what happens within the chamber boundaries. During this project I will be following a templete that I will produce by sketching.

Scenarios

Since this is a small town there will be simple things for events. A bake sale, a charity walk, and perhaps a firemans parade. There will be some sort of directory for the important postions in town. I will provide some sort of scale reguarding age or population as I have not decided yet.
